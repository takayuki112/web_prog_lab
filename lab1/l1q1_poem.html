My Poem...

Beneath the sky, so wide and blue...

Beneath the sky, so wide and blue,
The world turns soft, the air feels new.
A whisper stirs the trees in rhyme,
A quiet song that beats in time.

The sun sets low, the stars awake,
A peaceful calm the night will take.
And in the dark, so deep, so vast,
The moments pass, but they will last.

The moon rises high, a silver glow,
Lighting the path where quiet rivers flow.
In stillness, time begins to freeze,
As if the world is at perfect ease.

The wind begins to softly sigh,
Whispering tales as birds fly.
The stars above start to twinkle bright,
Casting shadows in the still of the night.

The flowers bloom in colors bright,
Bathing in the soft moon's light.
The air is fresh, the world feels right,
As dreams take flight in peaceful night.

A distant owl begins to call,
Its voice echoing, soft but tall.
The leaves rustle in the cool breeze,
A perfect sound that puts the heart at ease.

As dawn arrives with golden ray,
The night gives way to the bright new day.
The stars fade out, the sky turns blue,
And everything begins again, fresh and new.
